About Us

Our goal at BITS is to help impact the USC community through technology while helping new developers gain a valuable learning experience, and adept programmers some leadership experience. Essentially, members will form teams and work on projects that are intended to impact the life and experience on campus. Ideally, we would like to pair more experienced developers, with ones that have a bit less experience in order to create a more holistic learning environment. We would like to provide a development space for our members which mirrors the development processes in industry as closely as possible. Furthermore, we want to hold workshops as well as networking events to hopefully get our members acclimated to the tech industry.

As mentioned above, our main focus is on developing applications that will help the USC community. If you have an idea for an application that you think would help the USC community directly or indirectly, then you can pitch it at the general meeting and see your ideas come to life. We are looking for self motivated individuals to take initiative on projects and meet the deadlines that they set. Email us with more questions!
